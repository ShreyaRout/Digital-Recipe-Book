Breakfast

Breakfast sets the tone for the day, and our collection of recipes is designed to offer the perfect balance between taste and nutrition. Whether you're in a rush or have time to enjoy a relaxing morning, you'll find a variety of wholesome, easy-to-make options that will leave you energized and ready to take on the day. Here, you'll find a variety of breakfast recipes, perfect for starting your day with a healthy meal that both satisfies your cravings and supports your well-being.

Besan Chilla

Besan chilla is a savory Indian pancake made from besan (gram flour) mixed with spices, herbs, and water to form a smooth batter. It's quick to prepare and can be enjoyed as a healthy breakfast or snack.

Oats Bowl

Oats Bowl is a wholesome breakfast made from cooked oats, topped with a variety of fruits, nuts, seeds, and sweeteners. It's a nutritious and filling option rich in fiber and minerals.

Rava Idli

Rava idli is a steamed South Indian dish made from semolina, yogurt, and spices. It's light, fluffy, and often served with chutney and sambar.

Sunny Side Up with Toasts

Sprout Salad
